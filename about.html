We are a group of 5 friends who study the same engineering. We all share a passion for video games and we consider ourselves passionate and lucky to have the joy of studying this career.

About Us

Stumbles started a few months after the pandemic, with a group of 7 members: 2 of them did not continue studying, but the rest of us stayed together.

Among those 5 members are John Bruno and Miguel Aquino, currently working as technicians. The remaining 3 with Cristian Hernandez, Cesar Ruiz and Humberto Cerezo, a trio that was working as QA Testers. Thanks to the knowledge acquired during the career we were able to develop this video game.

An honorable mention is that most of the development of the game was possible thanks to Cristian's outstanding programming skills. Without him and his skills we would not have been able to finish all this on time.
